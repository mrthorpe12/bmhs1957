Beloit Memorial Class of '57

Welcome Classmates and friends. We are so pleased that you found us on the web. We hope you find this site informative and helpful in keeping in touch with high school classmates and old friends.

In these pages we will attempt to keep you informed of plans for our various events and reunions. You can find information on the planned events on the "planned events" page.

The "classmates" page can be used to keep track of old friends and remember those that have left us. You might also enjoy the photos pages of some of our previous reunions.
